# Wireless debugging to device
Device > Developer options:
    - USB debugging: On
    - Wireless ADB debugging: On

Connect device to USB for the first time, accept computer
In terminal:
adb kill-server
adb tcpip 5555
Unplug USB

Connect to device in terminal:
adb tcpip 5555
adb connect 192.168.0.121:5555
adb devices

Disconnect:
adb disconnect 192.168.0.121:5555
adb kill-server
